Site Plan - “ALOW”

WDD231 | Carlos Junco - Final Project

1. Site Name

Site Name: ALOW

Reason: “ALOW” is my personal DJ brand. It is short, modern, easy to remember, and visually strong—ideal for branding, social media presence, and event promotion. It reflects my artistic identity and musical style.

Optional Domain Suggestion: alowdj.com

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to professionally present my work and services as a DJ. The website aims to attract new clients, provide credibility, and showcase recent event media.

The site includes:

  • Information about the DJ services offered (weddings, private parties, clubs, corporate events).
  • A gallery with images and videos from recent performances.
  • Pricing or package estimates.
  • A contact form to request bookings or quotes.
  • A short biography and artistic description.

3. Scenarios

  • How much does it cost to hire ALOW for a private event like a wedding or a party?
  • Where can I see photos or videos from recent events to understand his style and experience?
  • What type of music and services does he offer? Does he include sound and lighting?

4. Color Scheme

This color palette reflects a modern, nightlife-inspired aesthetic that aligns with electronic music and DJ performance environments.

  • Black (#0F0F0F): Used for headers, section backgrounds, and navigation. Creates a professional, sleek look.
  • Neon Purple (#A020F0): Main accent color for buttons, titles, and essential highlights.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for text and high-contrast elements.
  • Light Gray (#f4f4f4): Used as the overall background for clean contrast.

5. Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings (h1, h2, h3). Modern, bold, and visually appealing.
  • Roboto: Used for body text. Clean, minimalistic, and easy to read.
